      <div class="hero__text">
        <h1>Jiaoyi Zhang</h1>
        <p class="hero__role">
          Assistant Professor, School of Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University
        </p>
        <p class="hero__summary">
          I am an Assistant Professor at the <a href="https://dase.ecnu.edu.cn/">School of Data Science and Engineering</a>, East China Normal University. My research focuses on database systems, indexing structures, AI infrastructure, and data security. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from the <a href="https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/">Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences</a>, Tsinghua University, where I was very fortunate to be advised by <a href="https://people.iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/~huanchen/">Prof. Huanchen Zhang</a>. Before that, I obtained my bachelor's degree from <a href="https://encc.nankai.edu.cn/">Nankai University</a>.
        </p>

        <div class="notice">
          <strong>Recruiting</strong>
          <p>
            I am looking for highly self-motivated Ph.D. students, Master students, and research interns (RAs). If you are interested in databases and systems research, please feel free to reach out.
          </p>
        </div>
